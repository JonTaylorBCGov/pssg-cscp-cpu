Calculations for Total Amount

The total amount will be calculated based on the rate that the counsellor on file is registered for. For reference, the calculation will be as follows:

Rate Session Hours Total
$40n hours$40 x n hrs = total
$60n hours$60 x n hrs = total
$80n hours$80 x n hrs = total
$95n hours$95 x n hrs = total
$105n hours$105 x n hrs = total
$15n hours$15 x n hrs = total

If all the above items you have entered are approved you will be eligible to receive the amount calculated from the hours submitted.
